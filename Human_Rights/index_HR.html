    HUMAN RIGHTS

    LGBTQ+ Minorities

    The LGBTQ+ minority is one of the most discriminated minorities to ever exist. LGBTQ+ youth are 62% more likely to commit suicide,do drugs,be exploited, or be sexually abused. LGBTQ+ youth are also bullied and discriminated in school and work, but only 1/2 of all the states have any laws against it. There has also been studies showing that 20% of LGBTQ+ believe that they have experienced unfair treatment in job interviews making life much harder for them. This stops now!

    Gender Minorities

    Women and transgender individuals are also heavily discriminated against. A study in 2017 showed that women make a average of $770 per week and men make about $941 per week. This is because of stereotypes putting women in lower paying jobs and because of the wage gap. Women earn aproximately 80% of what men earn even if theyre in the same job. A study suggests that transgender adults in the U.S. are 4 times more likely to have a income of less than 10,000 per year when compared the general population. Another suggests that 90% of transgender people have been harassed or put down because of theyre identity. Gender discrimination needs to be stopped.

    Minority Rights!

    Minorities are a group of people who expereince disadvantages as compared to the general population. Minorities also often suffer discrimination in they're everyday life. Minorities are based on ethnicity, race, religeon, sexual orientation, disabilities, and gender identity. Minorities deserve equality, participation in political rights, and safety/protection from discrimination.
